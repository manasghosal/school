DENOBILI MISSION SCHOOL is an educational institute, run through a trust. Its courses are based on C.B.S.E, It provides education through the medium of English, as it is affiliated to CBSE (New Delhi) till class X. The only aim of DENOBILI mission is to educate the children of rural areas & bring them parallel to big city nurture children.
Along with the day classes' school also provides hostel facility to the student. Boarders are always in care of supervisor. As we are the guardian of boarders we provide special teaching to them.
